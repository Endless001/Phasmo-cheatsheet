Phasmophobia: Ghosts and Updates

About The Game

Phasmophobia is the fear of ghosts or supernatural entities, often associated with intense anxiety or panic when confronted with thoughts or situations involving spirits or hauntings. People with phasmophobia may experience symptoms like rapid heartbeat, sweating, trembling, and shortness of breath when faced with anything related to the paranormal, such as visiting supposedly haunted locations or watching ghost-related media.

Treatment options for phasmophobia can include cognitive-behavioral therapy, exposure therapy, and relaxation techniques to help individuals manage and overcome their fear.

Ghosts

Spirit

Description: The most common type of ghost with no special abilities.

Strength: None.
Weakness: Using Smudge Sticks near a Spirit prevents it from hunting for a long time (180 seconds).
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, Ghost Writing.

Wraith

Description: One of the most dangerous ghosts, known for its ability to teleport to players.

Strength: It never touches the ground, so it leaves no footprints.
Weakness: Salt repels Wraiths, making them avoid it.
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, DOTS Projector.

Phantom

Description: A ghost that can disappear when taking a photo of it.

Strength: Looking at a Phantom significantly drops your sanity.
Weakness: Taking a photo makes it vanish temporarily.
Evidence: Spirit Box, Fingerprints, DOTS Projector.

Poltergeist

Description: Known for its ability to manipulate multiple objects at once.

Strength: Capable of throwing many objects simultaneously, creating fear.
Weakness: Useless in empty rooms with no objects to throw.
Evidence: Spirit Box, Fingerprints, Ghost Writing.

Hantu

Description: Moves faster in colder areas.

Strength: Increases speed in freezing temperatures.
Weakness: Slower in warmer areas.
Evidence: Fingerprints, Ghost Orb, Freezing Temperatures.

Yokai

Description: Becomes aggressive when players talk near it.

Strength: Hunts more frequently if players talk nearby.
Weakness: Limited hearing range during a hunt.
Evidence: Spirit Box, Ghost Orb, DOTS Projector.

Goryo

Description: Rarely seen and only appears on DOTS through cameras.

Strength: Does not show itself on DOTS unless viewed through a video camera.
Weakness: Rarely leaves its room.
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Fingerprints, DOTS Projector.

Myling

Description: Known for being quieter during hunts.

Strength: Produces quieter footsteps and sounds.
Weakness: More active with paranormal sounds on a Parabolic Microphone.
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Fingerprints, Ghost Writing.

Onryo

Description: Known as the “fire ghost.”

Strength: Will attack when flames are extinguished.
Weakness: Fears fire and is less likely to hunt when a flame is nearby.
Evidence: Spirit Box, Ghost Orb, Freezing Temperatures.

The Twins

Description: Two entities that act as one.

Strength: Can confuse players by interacting in different locations.
Weakness: Deciphering twin interactions can make them easier to identify.
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, Freezing Temperatures.

Raiju

Description: A ghost that feeds on electrical equipment.

Strength: Becomes faster around electronic devices.
Weakness: Disrupts electronic equipment, making it easier to track.
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Ghost Orb, DOTS Projector.

Obake

Description: Shapeshifters that are difficult to track.

Strength: Sometimes leaves unique fingerprints.
Weakness: Can shape-shift, leaving clues to its identity.
Evidence: EMF Level 5, Fingerprints, Ghost Orb.

The Mimic

Description: Copies the traits of other ghosts.

Strength: Can mimic another ghost’s strengths and abilities.
Weakness: Always leaves Ghost Orb evidence in addition to its other clues.
Evidence: Spirit Box, Fingerprints, Freezing Temperatures.
